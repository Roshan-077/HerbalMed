About

Lavender is a versatile plant known for its fragrant flowers. It's used in aromatherapy, cooking, and as an ornamental plant. Its calming scent promotes relaxation, while its delicate flavor enhances various dishes. Lavender is cherished for its beauty and attracts pollinators, making it popular in gardens.
